Innovating Fire Protection with AVYNAL ABFS

AVYNAL ABFS offers a revolutionary approach to fire protection, utilizing a special canister called an Aerosol Generating Unit (AGU). When triggered by fire detection system or inbuilt temperature sensor, the AGU releases a fine mist of fire-extinguishing particles. These particles act in two ways to suppress the fire:
