Industrial Applications

Aerosol fire suppression systems are extensively utilized in industrial settings for their ability to rapidly extinguish fires
and minimize damage to equipment and facilities.

Machinery
spaces

Protects critical machinery like CNC machines, generators, and robotic equipment from fire damage, preventing costly downtime and ensuring operational continuity.

Electrical
switchgear

Safeguards electrical control panels and distribution systems by rapidly suppressing fires, preventing short circuits, and protecting valuable infrastructure.

Hazardous storage areas

Ideal for storing flammable or combustible liquids and gases, offering effective fire protection and minimizing the risk of explosions.

Scientific environments

Protects laboratories, research facilities, and cleanrooms from fires, safeguarding personnel, equipment, and valuable research materials.

High-hazard ATEX areas

Specifically designed for environments with explosive atmospheres, ensuring safety and compliance with stringent regulations.

Commercial Applications

Aerosol fire suppression systems are becoming a popular choice in commercial environments where safeguarding
lives, protecting assets, and maintaining uninterrupted operations are top priorities.

Server rooms and switch rooms

Protects critical IT infrastructure, preventing data loss, system failures, and business disruptions.

Warehouses and storage facilities

Ideal for large-scale storage areas, offering rapid fire suppression, minimal water damage, and reduced downtime.

Vehicle
compartments

Protects vehicles, including cars, trucks, buses, and agricultural machinery, from fire-related damage and loss.

Other Applications

Aerosol fire suppression systems are applied in various specialized settings where conventional fire suppression
methods may not be suitable or effective.

Lithium-ion battery storage

Effectively suppresses fires in lithium-ion battery packs, preventing thermal runaway and explosions.

Storage of hazardous substances

Protects storage areas for various chemicals and materials, reducing the risk of fire spread and minimizing environmental impact.

Storage rooms and goods lifts

Safeguards valuable items and equipment in storage areas, preventing loss and damage.

Clean
Rooms

Maintains a clean environment while providing effective fire protection, suitable for industries with stringent cleanliness requirements.

Data
centers

Protects sensitive electronic equipment, servers, and network infrastructure from fire damage.

Offshore
platforms

Designed to withstand harsh marine environments, offering reliable fire protection for offshore operations.

Aviation


Protects aircraft components and passengers from fire hazards, enhancing safety in the aviation industry.
